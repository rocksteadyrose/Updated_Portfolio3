About Leslie

Leslie is a former social media manager for the number one kids’ music brand and a current web developer who is especially passionate about the front-end of applications.

She's a detail-oriented, highly creative consultant with almost a decade of experience working remotely.

Another passion of Leslie's is Children's Media, and she received her Master’s in Children’s Media from NYU. While living in New York City, she got to study with children’s media industry leaders, intern and work at companies like Sesame Street, and even be a balloon handler in the Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade. Since her time at NYU, Leslie has been creating a children's television show targeted at 4 to 8-year-old children to help develop their music literacy, which she will begin pitching later this year.

Leslie has always had very strong skills with software and computers and will be seeking a role as a web developer beginning in summer 2018. She hopes to further expand her skill sets at a company where she can continue to learn and excel while making a positive impact.
