About Leslie

I'm a former social media manager for the number one kids’ music brand and a current web developer who is especially passionate about the front-end of applications. I'm also a detail-oriented, highly creative consultant with almost a decade of experience working remotely.

Another passion of mine is Children's Media, and I received my Master’s in Children’s Media from NYU. While living in New York City, I had the privelege of studying with children’s media industry leaders, interning and working at companies like Sesame Workshop, and even being a balloon handler in the Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade. Since my time at NYU, I have been creating a children's television show targeted at 4 to 8-year-old children to help develop their music literacy, which I will begin pitching later this year.

I have always had very strong skills with software and computers and will be seeking a role as a web developer beginning in summer 2018. I hope to further expand my skill sets at a company where I can continue to learn and excel while making a positive impact.
